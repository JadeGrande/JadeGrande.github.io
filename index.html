--- layout: default title: Personal Webpage author: Hyun Seok Choi date: Apr 2025 abstract: In an era where visual sophistication often takes precedence over usability, many personal and portfolio websites prioritize aesthetics at the expense of intuitive navigation. This project introduces a static website that strikes a balance between creativity and clarity, built upon the widely respected template by Hamish (2020). By favoring a design that emphasizes readability, logical structure, and ease of use, the website challenges the trend of overly elaborate interfaces. The result is a digital presence that remains visually appealing while significantly enhancing user experience through intuitive interaction patterns and minimal cognitive load. ---

1   Introduction

Choi (1995) received his B.S degree from Seoul National University in 2021. After that, he has been pursuing a Ph.D at the Graduate School of Convergence Science and Technology, Seoul National University. He is a mamber of the Music and Audio Research Group(MARG), focusing on the research on the intersection of audio signal processing and machine learning.
